## Synopsis

Content from a global network of contributors—spanning images, videos, music, 3D models, and associated metadata—presents unique challenges in localization. This session explores how to scale multimedia content internationalization from supply through delivery to meet the demands of a global audience while maintaining cultural relevance and efficiency. Through real-world examples, we’ll highlight strategies to navigate bias in both machine and human processes and ensure high-quality, inclusive experiences. Attendees will gain insights into how localization enhances content discoverability, engagement, and growth in international markets.

## Key Takeaways

- Scalable approaches to localizing global contributor content across diverse formats
- Practices for cultural relevance and mitigating bias in AI and human-driven processes
- How metadata localization fuels discoverability and engagement in global markets
